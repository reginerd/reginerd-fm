What is this?

REGINERD-FM is reginerd's personal radio station — real music from a Plex library, one DJ (reginerd), and AI-generated talk breaks that sound like reginerd talking about tracks he actually loves. Music-forward. Bay Area energy. Running 24/7.

How it works

  • A Plex library on a NAS feeds real music into genre-tagged blocks across the day — Rap, R&B, Soul, Jazz, VGM, and more.
  • An LLM (Claude) writes short DJ breaks in reginerd's voice — track intros, deep cuts, context on artists and albums.
  • ElevenLabs (voice clone) renders the scripts to audio.
  • An ezstream + feeder.py pipeline builds per-block playlists, interleaves talk breaks with music, and pipes everything to Icecast.
  • An operator daemon (Claude Code on a 15-minute loop) keeps content stocked and carries editorial continuity across runs.
